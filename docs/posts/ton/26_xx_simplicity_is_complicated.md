# Simplicity is Complicated

In case it is not apparent yet, I must admit that I use this blog primarily as a vehicle to research a concept that is unclear to me and in the end satisfy some weird curiosity.
And this time, the concept is _simplicity_.

It fascinates me, because it is really hard to grasp. 
One the one hand I admire simple things.
Especially, when an otherwise complicated topic becomes apparent and clear, this is something that fills me with genuine joy.
On the other hand, things that are too simple to begin with can feel wrong if you assume you are not given the whole truth.
And doubting an ideas simplicity can destroy someone's world completely.

I remember a conversation with a colleague about what kind of research papers I like to write.
The ideal engineering-science paper for me is based on one simple idea.
This way it is easy to explain on the limited pages, and of course it will only be a good engineering paper if it manages to demonstrate that it is very effective at solving the addressed engineering problem.
But my co-worker answered that I must be careful with this, because reviews can be very challenging.
